Recipe #1: Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 24 Servings
Ingredients Quantity
White sugar 2 cups
All-purpose flour 1 3/4 cups
Unsweetened cocoa powder 3/4 cup
Baking powder 1 1/2 teaspoons
Baking soda 1 1/2 teaspoons
Salt 1 teaspoons
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
Boiling water 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9" round pans.

Step 2: In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of the mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

Step 3: Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven until the cake tests done with a toothpick reveal the cake is properly baked. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 4: Stack the two cake pieces together, and decorate it with cream and any toppings you want.

Recipe #2: Lemon Bars

  • Time: 55 minutes
  • Serves: 36 Servings
Ingredients Quantity
Softened butter 1 cup
White sugar 1/2 cup
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Eggs 4
White sugar 1 1/2 cups
All-purpose flour 1/4 cup
Juiced lemons 2

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.

Step 2: In a medium bowl, blend together softened butter, 2 cups flour and 1/2 cup sugar. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13 inch pan.

Step 3: Bake for 15-20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until firm and golden. In another bowl, whisk together the remaining 1 1/2 cups sugar and 1/4 cup flour. Whisk in the eggs and lemon juice. Pour over the baked crust.

Step 4: Bake for an additional 20 minutes in the preheated oven. The bars will firm up as they cool. After both pans have cooled, cut into uniform 2 inch squares and arrange in a checker board fashion.

Recipe #3: Pumpkin Pie

  • Time: 55 minutes
  • Serves: 10 Servings
Ingredients Quantity
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Ground cinnamon 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon
Ground cloves 1/4 teaspoon
Large eggs 2
Pumpkin Puree 1 can or 15 oz
Evaporated milk 1 can or 12 fl oz.
Unbaked 9" deep-dish pie shell 1

Step 1: Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves in a small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture. Gradually stir in evaporated milk.

Step 2: Pour mixture into pie shell.

Step 3: Bake in preheated 425 degrees F oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F; bake for 40 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
